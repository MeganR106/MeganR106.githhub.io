About Me

It's me! I'm the coder

I am currently in a software engineering program with Hack Reactor. I have joined an awesome community there where we have worked together to help each other, collaborate on different strategies, and be there for each when we're feeling discouraged. As a former teacher, I learned that your best tool is the people you work with and the community you build. Teaching also opened my eyes to how much I love to learn and research. When the pandemic hit, it forced me to turn to technology and create classwork online for my students. That was my taste for coding; it was some light html but I wanted to do more of it and learn more about it, which is why I joined Hack Reactor. Although things haven't come easy, I am persistent. I don't like keeping things unfinished. When I have a task to do; I see it through to the end which is why I would like to be part of a team that is getting things done but also working as a team. I want to join a team that not only allows me to grow as a software engineer but everyone else on that team as well.
